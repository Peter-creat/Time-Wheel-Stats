Time Wheel Stats is a focused app built with care. I’m a beginner developer, but I work hard to ship improvements and keep everything transparent for users and App Store reviewers.

What I’m building

My goal is to deliver real value through thoughtful design, smooth performance, and privacy. The app is made to be easy to use and to respect the user.

  • Clarity: simple screens, readable results, and predictable behavior.
  • Stability: careful testing and frequent small improvements.
  • Privacy: no analytics, no tracking, no data collection.
  • Listening: user feedback directly shapes future updates.
